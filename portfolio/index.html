I created this project with the idea to demonstrate code re-use along with MVP architecture and this has been proven to be valuable approach. You'll find more about that in github repository.

Go Ubiquitous

November 2016

A watch face is what's shown when screen is on, and no other apps are running. It's the first thing you will see when you glance at your watch. It goes without saying that watch face show the time and it can also be a highly functional way to share concise, timely information. Two major functionalities provided are:

  • Periodically update weather information from Handheld to Android wear.
  • Watch face that displays temperature along with weather condition.

From this project, our aim is to provide an application that will benefit patients in critical situations like requirement of a specific type of blood. This will reduce the overhead of finding a blood donor in a nearby location during emergency situations. Thus connecting blood donors and recipients is our prime focus.
